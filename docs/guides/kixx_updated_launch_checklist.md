## üö® TROUBLESHOOTING COMMON ISSUES

### Problem: Website crashes on launch day
**Solution:** 
- Use reliable hosting (Shopify handles this automatically)
- Test website under load before launch
- Have tech support on standby

### Problem: Not enough email subscribers before launch
**Solution:**
- Run Facebook ads to landing page with signup incentive
- Offer 15% off for early subscribers
- Partner with complementary brands for email list swaps

### Problem: Low engagement on social media
**Solution:**
- Post more video content (4x more engagement than images)
- Engage with other accounts (comment, like, share)
- Run giveaway contest (free KiXX Square to one winner)
- Use trending hashtags, not just niche ones

### Problem: High cart abandonment rate
**Solution:**
- Send abandoned cart email sequence
- Reduce friction in checkout (guest checkout option)
- Show trust badges (secure checkout, money-back guarantee)
- Offer free shipping over certain amount

### Problem: Negative feedback or returns
**Solution:**
- Respond quickly and professionally
- Offer full refund, no questions asked
- Ask for specific feedback to improve
- Turn negative into positive (public resolution)
