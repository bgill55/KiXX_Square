## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Challenges & Solutions

**1. Low Initial Sales**
- **Solution:** Increase ad spend, offer limited-time 20% discount, reach out to more influencers

**2. Negative Reviews/Product Issues**
- **Solution:** Respond immediately, offer replacements, address design flaws in next batch, highlight positive reviews

**3. Copycat Competitors**
- **Solution:** Leverage patent-pending status, emphasize "original," build brand loyalty fast, continue innovation

**4. High Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
- **Solution:** Focus on organic content, referral program, improve conversion rate, target warmer audiences

**5. Inventory Shortage**
- **Solution:** Waitlist system, pre-orders for next batch, communicate transparently about restock dates

**6. Fulfillment Delays**
- **Solution:** Partner with 3PL, set clear shipping expectations, offer tracking, over-communicate
