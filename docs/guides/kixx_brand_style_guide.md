## Logo Usage Guidelines

### Logo Placement
Your KiXX Square logo should be placed in these positions on marketing materials:

**Website/Digital:**
- Top left corner (standard web convention)
- Centered on hero banners for impact
- Bottom right on social posts for branding

**Print Materials:**
- Top center on product packaging
- Top left on documents and flyers
- Watermark on product photos (subtle, 20% opacity)

### Logo Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space around logo equal to the height of the "K" in KIXX:
- Minimum 50px on digital assets
- Minimum 5mm on print materials

## How to Add Your Logo & Product Photos

### Using Adobe Photoshop

1. **Open the branded graphic** in Photoshop
2. **Locate the logo placeholder** (white rectangle/circle with "KIXX" text)
3. **Create a new layer** above the placeholder
4. **Place your logo** (File > Place Embedded)
5. **Resize and position** to fit within the placeholder area
6. **Delete the placeholder layer** or hide it
7. **Save as JPEG** with quality 90-95

### Using Canva (Free Alternative)

1. **Upload the branded graphic** to Canva
2. **Upload your logo** to Canva
3. **Drag your logo** onto the canvas
4. **Position it** over the placeholder
5. **Resize** using corner handles (hold Shift to maintain aspect ratio)
6. **Delete or cover** the placeholder using a rectangle shape
7. **Download as JPG** (high quality)

### Using GIMP (Free Open Source)

1. **Open the branded graphic** in GIMP
2. **Open your logo** as a new layer (File > Open as Layers)
3. **Use the Move tool** to position your logo
4. **Scale if needed** (Layer > Scale Layer)
5. **Delete the placeholder** using selection and delete
6. **Export as JPEG** (File > Export As)

### Product Photo Integration Tips

**Best Practices:**
- Use high-resolution photos (at least 2000px on longest side)
- Ensure good lighting - bright, even, minimal shadows
- Clean white or neutral background preferred
- Show the product from multiple angles
- Include "in-use" action shots on job sites
- Show scale - hands holding it, next to common lumber

**Photo Placement:**
- **Hero Banner:** Right side, 40% of width, slight angle
- **Facebook Ad:** Center or right side, facing left
- **Instagram Post:** Center, with logo above or below
- **Feature Highlights:** Close-up of specific feature in "PRODUCT PHOTO" box
- **Stories:** Vertical orientation, 50-60% of frame height
