Built by professionals, for professionals

Stop Measuring. Start Marking.

Six powerful features engineered for professional carpenters

Tired of juggling a tape measure, calculator, and three different squares just to frame a wall? The KiXX Square is the all-in-one layout tool engineered for professional carpenters. We built in the measurements, the math, and the shortcuts so you can work faster, reduce errors, and ditch the extra tools.

See exactly how each feature saves you time on the job.

1

Frame Walls Faster

Forget the guesswork. The KiXX Square features large, cut-away notches and V-grooves for rapid stud layouts.

Key Benefits:

  • Built-in notches at 3½" and 5½" for instant 2x4 and 2x6 marking
  • V-groove path with marks at 1½", 3", and 4½" for trimmer studs
  • No more measuring each stud individually
  • Eliminate calculation errors on wall layouts
See How-To Guide →
2

Nail Your Anchor Bolts

Get your drill position right the first time. The KiXX Square has a notched spine that fits around the anchor bolt, letting you mark the exact center.

Key Benefits:

  • Notched spine fits snugly around the bolt
  • Specific holes for 3½" (2x4) and 5½" (2x6) materials
  • Mark exact drill position every time
  • No more off-center holes or wasted lumber
See How-To Guide →
3

Draw Parallel Lines with Ease

Our Progressive Scribe feature makes it simple to draw lines parallel to the edge of a board.

Key Benefits:

  • Marks for measurements from ¼" all the way up to 5¾"
  • Hook the spine against the edge and slide
  • Perfect for scribing reveals, offsets, and trim work
  • Consistent parallel lines every time
See How-To Guide →
4

Get an Instant 22.5° Angle Shortcut

For common cuts, speed is everything. The removable ¼" threaded pin acts as a physical stop to get an immediate 22.5° angle or 5/12 roof pitch.

Key Benefits:

  • Removable pin screws into stationary position
  • Perfect 22.5-degree angle every time
  • Ideal for octagon cuts and trim work
  • No protractor or calculations needed
See How-To Guide →
5

Mark Rafters in a Single Pass

Marking a perfect bird's mouth is no longer a multi-step process. Our "Hip & Val" system simplifies it.

Key Benefits:

  • Single-pass marking for rafter seat cuts
  • Seat Cut Pivot point for precise positioning
  • Hip or Valley marks for different roof pitches
  • Trace the seat cut line in one motion
See How-To Guide →
6

Use the Built-In Roof Math

Leave the calculator in the truck. The spine of the KiXX Square is etched with the complex roof geometry tables you actually need on the job.

Key Benefits:

  • Rafter Length adjustments at 24" O.C.
  • Hip/Valley diagonal layout numbers
  • 48" sheathing cut measurements
  • All roof pitches clearly marked on spine
See How-To Guide →
