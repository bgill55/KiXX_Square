Return & Refund Policy

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Our Promise

We're confident the KiXX Square will transform your workflow. If you're not completely satisfied, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

Return Window

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to return your KiXX Square for a full refund.

Return Requirements

To be eligible for a return, your KiXX Square must be:

  • In original condition (unused or gently used)
  • In the original packaging
  • Accompanied by all original materials
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery

How to Start a Return

  1. Contact Us: Email returns@kixxsquare.com with your order number
  2. Get Authorization: We'll send you a Return Authorization (RA) number
  3. Pack It Up: Place the KiXX Square in original packaging
  4. Ship It Back: Use the prepaid label we provide or your preferred carrier

Return Shipping

Changed Your Mind?

  • Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping service
  • Insure the package for the full value

Defective or Damaged Product?

  • We'll provide a prepaid return label at no cost
  • Contact us within 48 hours of receiving a damaged product
  • Provide photos of the damage

Refund Processing

Once we receive your return:

  • Inspection: We'll inspect the product (1-2 business days)
  • Approval: If approved, refund is processed immediately
  • Refund Time: Refunds appear in 5-10 business days
  • Original Payment Method: Refund goes to your original payment method

What's Refunded

  • ✓ Full product price ($199.99)
  • ✓ Original shipping (if product was defective)
  • ✗ Return shipping costs (unless defective)

Exchanges

Need to exchange a defective product?

  • Contact us immediately
  • We'll send a replacement at no charge
  • Return the defective unit with prepaid label
  • Exchanges ship within 1-2 business days

Warranty Coverage

The KiXX Square includes a 1-year warranty against manufacturing defects:

  • Covers material and workmanship defects
  • Does not cover normal wear and tear
  • Does not cover misuse or modifications
  • Contact us for warranty claims

Non-Returnable Items

We cannot accept returns on:

  • Products damaged due to misuse
  • Products modified or altered
  • Products returned after 30 days
  • Products without proof of purchase
