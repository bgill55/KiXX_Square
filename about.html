About KiXX Square

Engineered by professionals, for professionals

Built by Carpenters Who Know the Trade

The KiXX Square was born from real frustration on real job sites. After years of watching talented carpenters waste time juggling multiple tools, constantly calculating the same measurements, and dealing with layout errors that cost time and materials, we knew there had to be a better way.

We didn't just design a tool—we engineered a solution. Every feature on the KiXX Square was developed to solve a specific problem that professional carpenters face every single day:

  • Wall framing delays? We built in the stud layout notches.
  • Anchor bolt headaches? We created the precision hole-marking system.
  • Complex roof calculations? We etched the math right onto the spine.
  • Repetitive angle cuts? We added the 22.5° threaded pin shortcut.

Our Mission

To eliminate wasted time and reduce errors on job sites by providing professional carpenters with intelligently designed, all-in-one layout tools that combine the functionality of multiple traditional tools into a single, precision-engineered solution.
